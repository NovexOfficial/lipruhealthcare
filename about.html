About Lipru Healthcare

Your trusted partner for home diagnostic services. Bringing certified lab testing right to your doorstep in Virar, Mumbai, and beyond.

Why We Started

The idea of Lipru Healthcare was born from a simple observation: getting a basic blood test done meant traveling, waiting in long queues, and losing productive hours. For senior citizens and busy professionals, this was a massive hurdle.

We decided to bridge this gap. By combining cutting-edge digital booking systems with highly trained certified phlebotomists, we created a platform where anyone can book a blood test in just 60 seconds and get it done from the comfort of their home, without any extra collection charges.

Our Mission

To deliver highly accurate, affordable, and hyper-convenient diagnostic services to every household, ensuring that preventive healthcare is accessible to all without compromising on quality.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and technologically advanced home healthcare platform, seamlessly integrating testing, nutrition, and medical store needs into one smart ecosystem.
