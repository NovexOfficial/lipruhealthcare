Frequently Asked Questions

Is home sample collection safe?

Yes, our certified phlebotomists follow strict WHO hygiene protocols, use single-use sterile kits, and ensure complete safety during the home visit.

When will I receive my reports?

Most standard blood test reports are delivered digitally via WhatsApp and Email within 24 hours of sample collection.

Do I need to fast before the test?

It depends on the test you select. Profiles like Diabetes or Lipid usually require 8-10 hours of fasting. Our team will guide you upon booking.
